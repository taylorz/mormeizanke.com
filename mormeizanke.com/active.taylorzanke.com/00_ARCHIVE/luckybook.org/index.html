Lucky Book

Lucky Book produces one of a kind books in single editions of limited number. The books strive to present alternative narratives of meaning and purpose for those individuals who make them. Hopefully, the books are close relatives of yearbooks, diaries, or hand-me-down novels. They daydream of being trusty, dependable, treasured, lucky.

The books aren't produced by professional presses, and printing is usually done on digital printers in apartments, bedrooms, or back-of-house spaces. As a result, these places are often also collaborators in the production of the work. Sometimes the subject matter of the books have something to do with where they are printed.

Lucky is run by artist Taylor Zanke.
